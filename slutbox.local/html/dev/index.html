Who we are

This totally unique subscription box by Amber Rose is an inclusive, feminist beauty box curated by MUVA herself to empower, uplift and celebrate everything that makes you sexy, unique and totally fierce.

Every month, let MUVA be your personal shopper, your stylist and your MUVA! Amber Rose’s box is hand-curated by MUVA herself to bring you the dopest luxury skincare, beauty, bodycare, wellness and style. Everything is hand picked and approved by Amber, so you know you’ll be gettin’ that good good. With each box we’re building a community of boss babes who stand in their strength, explore their confidence and flaunt their beauty. We stand for feminism, empowerment, and body-positivity - and we look pretty damn fly while we’re doin’ it.

A proud mother, activist, television and radio personality, producer, top-selling author of How to Be a Bad Bitch, model, entrepreneur...and now subscription box founder, Amber Rose’s multi-faceted personality, drive, and unapologetic voice fuel her meteoric rise.

With bodacious curves and a face that launched a million DMs, Amber has long been known for her striking beauty and her bold style.

A leading voice in championing body positivity, confidence and self-love, Amber Rose is an icon of the neo-feminist movement. MUVA might have started as just a pretty face, but now millions hear her voice. Amber Rose is proud to bring you the world’s first truly feminist and body positive beauty and lifestyle subscription box in the world.

While MUVA's empire continues to flourish, she is most proud of being a devoted mother to her son Sebastian Taylor Thomaz.

How It Works

We are a totally unique monthly subscription box focused on beauty, fashion, and wellness… all with a body positive, feminist twist.

With every box, Amber Rose and her team curate an experience designed to empower, uplift, educate and pamper. We’re inclusive here, so no matter how you identify or where you’re from, you can sit with us, babe!

Each box comes filled with carefully selected items to make you feel good from the inside out. We’ve got everything -- luxe body care, glam cosmetics, wellness essentials and dope wearables -- all hand-picked and approved by MUVA.
